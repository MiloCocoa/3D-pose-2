### Current Status (2026-03-30)
- **Architecture**: Dual-Head system (Rule-Based + ST-GCN) with Virtual Node Synthesis (Mid-Hip, Mid-Shoulder, Mid-Ear) is implemented in `src/rules/engine.py` and `src/inference.py`.
- **Rule-Based Head**: Tracks 6 deterministic labels (Depth, Knee Valgus, Hip Shift/Tilt, etc.) using geometric thresholds.
- **AI Head**: Specialized for 4 "Postural" labels (Thoracic, Trunk, Descent/Ascent Mechanics).
